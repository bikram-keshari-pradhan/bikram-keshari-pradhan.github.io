Neutron Star Gravitational Wave Asteroseismology

Solving for the Neutron Star Oscillation modes with different NS interior

Inferring NS interior using GW from isolated and binary NS system
with Current and XG GW detectors.

❮ ❯

Neutron Star Equation of State (EOS) Modelling

Determining the NS EoS with nucleons and/or exotic matter, analytically (involves nuclear physics, particle physics, hypernuclear physics), and numerically.

Imposing constraints from multidisciplinary Physics on nuclear and hypernuclear NS EoSs.
